About Ahmed Khalil & Fixbulance®

Fixbulance® was founded with a simple mission: to provide professional, convenient, and reliable mobile device repair services to the Orland Park community and surrounding areas.

Our owner, Ahmed Khalil, recognized the need for quality mobile repair services that come to you. Instead of waiting in long lines at repair shops or dealing with unreliable service, Fixbulance® brings certified technicians directly to your location.
